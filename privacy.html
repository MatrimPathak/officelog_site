Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Officelog is operated by Matrim Pathak. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Officelog mobile application.

Information We Collect

When users create an account using authentication providers such as Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, or Firebase Authentication, the following information may be collected:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture

User Data

The app may store the following information provided by the user:

  • Office location selected during onboarding
  • Attendance logs
  • Work hour or productivity logs
  • Holiday preferences

Location Usage

The app may access precise device location for the Auto Check-in feature using geofencing.

User location is never stored on servers. All geofence calculations occur locally on the device. Only the office location selected by the user is stored.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on services provided by Google Maps, Firebase, and Sign in with Apple.

Data Retention

User data is stored until the user deletes their account. When an account is deleted, all associated data is permanently removed.

Children’s Privacy

Officelog is not intended for users under the age of 13.
