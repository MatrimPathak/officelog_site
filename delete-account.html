Delete Your Officelog Account

Users can permanently delete their Officelog account directly from within the mobile application.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Officelog app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently removed
  • All attendance logs will be deleted
  • Office location information will be deleted
  • All productivity records will be deleted

Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.
